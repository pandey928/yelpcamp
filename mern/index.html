About Reacher

Jack Reacher, created by Lee Child, is a fictional ex-military police major. Standing at 6 feet 5 inches, he embodies physical strength, intelligence, and a strong moral code. A nomadic loner, Reacher roams from place to place, using his combat skills and analytical mind to right wrongs and dispense justice.
